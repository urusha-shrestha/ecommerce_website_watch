ROLEX SEA DWELLER

Introducing the new Rolex Oyster Perpetual Sea-Dweller in a Yellow Rolesor version, combining Oystersteel and 18 CT Yellow Gold. This new watch brings 18 CT Yellow Gold to the Sea-Dweller range for the first time.



Underwater Exploration Tool

Initially waterproof to a depth of 610 metres, the Sea-Dweller was designed in collaboration with the pioneers of professional deep-sea diving, who spent extended periods underwater. This ultra-resistant tool watch accompanied the first experimental underwater habitat programmes.
